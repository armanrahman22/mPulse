Can I use these kiosks instead of going to the doctor?
The data from M-Pulse kiosks is not meant as a replacement for regularly scheduled doctor's visits. Rather, it is meant to help you stay healthy by providing convenient and free health information that is accessible whenever you need it. M-Pulse kiosk data should be used in conjunction with professional health services, not as a replacement.
How can I setup my own kiosk?
Contact us in order to get set up as a developer. Once you are registered with us, you will have access to the hardware and software documentation for the kiosks.

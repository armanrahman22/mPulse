Overview

The purpose of this website is to provide M-Pulse kiosk users with a way to easily store and manage their health data. Here you can view your kiosk sessions, graph long-term trends, and export session data for offline storage, printing, or sharing.

View Sessions

Sessions can be viewed from your profile page.

Graph Data Over Time

You can view graphs of data by clicking on the icon wherever you see it.

Share Sessions

You can export or email session data as a PDF by going to the session page and clicking Download PDF or Email PDF at the bottom of the page. You can share, save, or print these PDFs however you would like.

Data Trends

Data from M-Pulse Kiosks is occasionally anonymously amassed and analyzed to produce graphs and analysis of local and global health trends. These can be found on the trends page.
